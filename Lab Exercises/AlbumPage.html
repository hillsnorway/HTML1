Star Turtle

Band Name: Harry Connick Jr.

Release Date: July 2, 1996

Star Turtle is Harry Connick, Jr.'s most ambitious album, a four-part suite that attempts to tell the evolution of jazz and R&B to funk and rock.

Band Members

Harry Connick Jr. - Vocals
Harry Connick Jr. - Piano
