Simple Recipes - Fry an Egg

The Ingredients

  • An egg
  • Butter (1 tbl. spoon)
  • Salt & Pepper

The Procedure

  1. Heat a frying pan on HIGH heat
  2. Add 1 tbl. spoon of butter
  3. Crack an egg into the middle of the frying pan
  4. Salt and pepper the fried egg to taste
    (Optional: Flip the egg)
  5. Remove the egg from the frying pan when the egg whites are firm
    (Optional: If you like the yolk firm, fry 15-20 seconds longer)
